    For me, respect is above all. Everyone should feel safe to express themselves. Without respect, there can be no trust.

  • Collaboration

    Everybody has unique strengths. We can only thrive when we evolve together to support each other.

  • Innovation

    There is no progress when we accept the status quo. We need to challenge what we know and tackle what we don't.

    I like to throw myself into the cold water and start a new project. Sometimes without having any knowledge about what I am doing - at least in the beginning. But solving problems and facing challenges is the thing in my life that makes me forget to sleep (sometimes).
